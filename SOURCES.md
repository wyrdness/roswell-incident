## Critical Reading Notes

When evaluating Roswell sources, consider:
- **Publication date**: Pre-1978 vs. post-1978 (resurgence period)
- **Witness testimony timing**: Contemporary (1947) vs. decades later
- **Author bias**: Believer, skeptic, or neutral investigator
- **Evidence standards**: Anecdotal vs. documented
- **Financial interests**: Book sales, tourism, media rights
